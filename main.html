Introducing Maunga Club: Your Gateway to Alpine Luxury and Adventure

Nestled amidst the breathtaking majesty of the mountains, the Maunga Club invites you to experience a world of unparalleled snow-capped wonder and alpine luxury.
Our exclusive club offers an extraordinary opportunity for avid snow enthusiasts and discerning travelers to immerse themselves in the ultimate mountain retreat.

Who Are We?

Our dedicated team is here to curate bespoke experiences, ensuring that every moment spent at Maunga Club is a cherished memory. We believe in creating extraordinary moments amidst the beauty of the mountains. Our exclusive club is more than just a destination; it's a sanctuary for those who seek to elevate their alpine experiences to new heights.
The Maunga Club provides access to a collection of meticulously designed snow and ski chalets, or cabins, strategically situated at the heart of the mountain. These charming abodes blend seamlessly into their natural surroundings, offering an intimate haven where you can unwind and recharge after exhilarating days on the slopes.

Our Chalets

Our collection of snow and ski chalets embodies the essence of refined mountain living. Cozy interiors adorned with rustic charm cater your every need, our chalets are designed to be your private oasis amidst the splendor of the outdoors

Join Us

As a member of Maunga Club, you become part of an exclusive community united by a shared love for the mountains. Whether you're a seasoned explorer or a first-time adventurer,
our club offers a haven where you can connect with nature, discover new passions, and create bonds that last a lifetime.We invite you to embark on a journey with us

To apply for Club Membership you'll need:

  • Your Full Name
  • Your Home Address
  • Proof of Your Address (Copy of power bill)
  • Proof of ID (Driver license or passport)

The above needs to be sent to the following Club Maunga email address: maungaclub@maungaclub.maungaclub

Membership will be confirmed by email. Additional information about the Club and accommodation (membership letter) will be sent by regular mail.
